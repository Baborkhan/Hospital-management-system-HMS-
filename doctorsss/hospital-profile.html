About Hospital

Rajshahi Medical College Hospital (RMCH) is one of the largest and oldest government hospitals in Northern Bangladesh. Established in 1958, it serves as the main tertiary care referral center for Rajshahi division and surrounding areas. The hospital has 1,000+ beds with state-of-the-art medical equipment and facilities.
