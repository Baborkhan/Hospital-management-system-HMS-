  • Content created by AI (MedFind AI) should be considered preliminary information only, not medical guidance

About Doctor Consultations

  • Doctors listed on MedFind are independent licensed professionals with BMDC registration
  • MedFind facilitates connections but does not supervise or control individual doctor-patient interactions
  • Telemedicine consultations have limitations — certain conditions require in-person examination
  • Always follow your doctor's advice and seek second opinions for serious diagnoses
  • Report any concerns about a doctor's conduct to us at medfindbd2026@gmail.com and to BMDC

About Drug & Medication Information

  • Drug information on MedFind is sourced from standard pharmacological references
  • Never start, stop, or change medications based solely on platform information
  • Drug interactions, contraindications, and dosing must be confirmed with your doctor or pharmacist
  • Medications available in Bangladesh may differ from international standards
  • Report adverse drug reactions to the Directorate General of Drug Administration (DGDA) Bangladesh

About MedFind AI (AI Symptom Checker)

  • The AI symptom checker uses Claude AI (Anthropic) for pattern recognition only
  • AI outputs are NOT medical diagnoses — they are informational suggestions to discuss with a doctor
  • AI systems can make errors and may not account for your complete medical history
  • Never delay seeking emergency care based on an AI response
  • Mental health queries should be directed to qualified mental health professionals

About Blood Donor Services

  • MedFind verifies donor registration but cannot verify current health eligibility

  • MedFind is not liable for any complications arising from blood donation or transfusion
