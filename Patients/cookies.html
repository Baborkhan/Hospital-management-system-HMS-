Cookies Policy

Understanding how MedFind uses cookies to improve your experience

Last Updated: April 2026

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help websites remember your preferences, keep you logged in, and understand how you use the site. MedFind uses cookies to deliver a better, more personalised healthcare experience.

Types of Cookies We Use

Cookie Type Purpose Duration Category
Authentication Keeps you logged in securely across sessions Session / 30 days Essential
Session Maintains your current browsing session state Session Essential
Preferences Remembers language and display preferences 1 year Essential
Analytics Tracks page views and usage patterns to improve the platform 90 days Analytics
Performance Monitors load times and error rates 30 days Analytics
Location Stores your last searched area to show nearby doctors 7 days Optional

Essential Cookies

These cookies are strictly necessary for the MedFind platform to function. Without them, core features like login, appointment booking, and secure payments will not work. These cannot be disabled.

Analytics Cookies

We use analytics cookies to understand how users interact with MedFind, which pages are most visited, and where users encounter issues. This data is aggregated and anonymous — it helps us improve the platform for everyone.

Managing Your Cookie Preferences

You can control non-essential cookies at any time:

  • Browser Settings: Most browsers allow you to view, delete, or block cookies. Check your browser's help documentation for instructions.
  • Account Settings: Logged-in users can manage cookie preferences in their profile settings.
  • Opt-Out: You may opt out of analytics cookies without affecting core functionality.

Note: Disabling essential cookies will affect your ability to use the platform.

Updates to This Policy

We may update this Cookies Policy as our platform evolves. We will notify you of significant changes via email or an in-app notification. Continued use of MedFind after changes constitutes acceptance.
