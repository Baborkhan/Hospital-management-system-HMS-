Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book an appointment?
Go to Doctor Search, select a doctor, choose an available slot, and confirm booking. You'll receive a confirmation.
Can I cancel my appointment?
Yes. Go to My Appointments in your dashboard and click Cancel. Refunds depend on the hospital's policy.
Is my medical data safe?
Yes. All medical records are stored securely. See our Privacy Policy for details.
Is MedFind AI a replacement for a real doctor?
No. MedFind AI provides general health guidance only. Always consult a qualified doctor for diagnosis and treatment.
